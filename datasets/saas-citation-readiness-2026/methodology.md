# AI Citation Readiness Benchmark for B2B SaaS Websites: Methodology

This public dataset summarizes observable citation-readiness signals from 50 B2B SaaS websites. It is designed as a reference for SEO, GEO, AEO, content, product marketing, and research teams that need a repeatable way to discuss whether public pages are easy for answer engines to understand and cite.

## What the benchmark measures

The benchmark checks public, observable website signals rather than private traffic, rankings, conversions, or model-training behavior.

Signals include:

- Crawlable homepage response.
- Presence of structured data on the homepage.
- Public pricing or plans page.
- About, company, or entity explanation page.
- Customer proof, case studies, or review-oriented proof.
- Resource hub or blog.
- Documentation or help center.
- Trust, security, or compliance page.
- Comparison or alternatives page.

Each brand receives a directional score based on these observable signals. The score is not a product-quality ranking and does not claim that an answer engine will cite a brand.

## Limitations

- The benchmark is based on public website observations from one crawl window.
- The dataset does not measure private analytics, buyer behavior, model training data, or actual inclusion inside a specific AI answer.
- Dynamic rendering, geolocation, bot protection, and site experiments may affect crawl results.
- The sample is directional and should not be treated as an exhaustive market ranking.
