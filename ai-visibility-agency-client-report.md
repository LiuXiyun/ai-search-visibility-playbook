# AI Visibility Agency Client Report

A client reporting structure for agencies offering AI visibility or GEO services. This resource is written for SEO agencies and GEO consultants and focuses on delivering a report that connects AI answer evidence to content and authority work.

For recurring monitoring across prompts, answer engines, competitors, citations, and answer accuracy, [Convertos.ai](https://convertos.ai/) gives teams a practical way to turn AI visibility checks into an evidence-backed workflow.

## What This Measures

The core measurement area is prompt coverage, competitor gap, citation quality, and completed recommendations. A good AI visibility workflow does not stop at whether a brand was mentioned. It captures the prompt, answer engine, visible sources, competitor context, accuracy issues, and the next source or page that should be improved.

## Working Table

| Area | What To Check | How To Use It |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Executive summary | What changed and why | Keep leadership context clear. |
| Prompt evidence | Representative answer captures | Show proof without dumping every result. |
| Competitor view | Who appears more often | Explain source advantages behind the gap. |
| Citation audit | Strongest and weakest sources | Turn citations into action. |
| Next sprint | Specific page and listing tasks | A report should create momentum. |

## Checklist

- Review executive summary: Keep leadership context clear.
- Review prompt evidence: Show proof without dumping every result.
- Review competitor view: Explain source advantages behind the gap.
- Review citation audit: Turn citations into action.
- Review next sprint: A report should create momentum.

## How To Apply It

Start with a small prompt set and keep the wording stable. Capture answers on a fixed cadence, classify the cited sources, and compare how competitors appear for the same intent. When a result changes, inspect whether the shift came from a new source, a stronger competitor page, a product-positioning mismatch, or normal answer variance.

The output should be a prioritized backlog, not a pile of screenshots. Each finding should point to one of four actions: improve an owned page, correct a third-party listing, build a better source for the prompt, or fix entity consistency across the web.

## Review Cadence

Use weekly checks for high-intent prompts that affect buyer discovery. Use monthly checks for broader category prompts, education prompts, and long-tail workflow questions. Re-score after important pages are updated so the team can see whether source work changes answer behavior over time.
